## Welcome to Diadna

Diadna is transforming the diamond industry with cutting-edge AI technology designed to **authenticate stones with unmatched accuracy**. Whether you're verifying, registering, or mapping stones, Diadna ensures your data is secure, reliable, and lightning fast — enabling you to make confident decisions every time.

With a strong focus on precision, security, and ease of use, Diadna empowers diamond manufacturers, traders, and verifiers to streamline their operations and stay ahead in a highly competitive market.

---

## What You Can Do with Diadna

- 🔍 **Stone Verification:** Instantly check if a stone matches with global authenticity patterns using our AI-powered verification engine.
- 📝 **Stone Registration:** Securely register stones to your company's portfolio to prevent duplicates and ensure traceability.
- 🧠 **Code Mapping Intelligence:** Automate and simplify the classification of your stones using Diadna’s intelligent code mapping tools.
- 🚀 **API-First Architecture:** Easily integrate Diadna into your internal systems and workflows with robust, well-documented APIs.
